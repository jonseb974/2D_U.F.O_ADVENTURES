FEATURES

In this is a 2D GUI video game, you mission takes place in the context of a space invader like shooting game.

In this game you are flying in a space craft, you have to secure the area_974. You have a solid shield you will not die in this game, it is a beat the clock challenge.

Many dangerous invaders pirates suicide drones are hunting around the dead moon. Use your weapons, collect the yellow items to increase your ammonition level, and power up your laser beams.
